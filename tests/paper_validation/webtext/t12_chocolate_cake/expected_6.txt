To prevent your layers from sticking together, I used my 8-inch round tart pan, lined it with parchment paper, then cut up 4 7-inch round cakes from it and sandwiched the layers between them (this also worked well for the 1-layer chocolate cake as well). When you're ready to bake this recipe, remove the parchment paper, preheat oven to 350 degrees and bake for about 30 – 40 minutes, until the top is golden and a tester comes out clean.
